Clear Slime

Simple basic, but cool!

For making claer slime, we need 1/2 cup PVA glue, 1 tablespoon saline solution which contain boric acid and sodium borate, 1/2 cup of Water, and 1/4-1/2 tsp baking soda.

Butter Slime

Creamy textrue and savory smell!

For the perfect butter slime,we need water glue, woodworking glue, Renew, shaving foam, baking soda, warm water, glitter and pen!

Fluffy slime

Marshmallow texture like cat fur!

Fluffy slime needs baking soda, saline solution, food color, foaming hand soap, whithe glue, and shaving cream!

Crunchy slime

It will help you to make ASMR sounds..!

For the awesome sounds of Crunchy slime, it needs 1/2 cup PVA glue, 1 tablespoon saline solution which contain boric acid and sodium borate, 1/2 cup of water, nice shape of beads,and 1/4-1/2 tsp baking soda!

Iceberg slime

It will provide you fantastic sound experience..!

Iceberg slime ingredients are same as Fluffy slime, but there is one more step for it. Leave the uncovered Fluffy slime in the fidge for a day or two! It will dry and you will be able to get crunch top!

Cloud slime

Do you wanna get unmeltable snow?

You can make cloud slime if you add instant snow in whatever slime you have!
